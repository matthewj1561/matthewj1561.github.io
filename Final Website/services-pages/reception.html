Reception Services

The perfect wedding

Temple Inn and Suites are specially suited to hosting large events for a variety of events. However, with the ideal locations of our hotels, we are perhaps best suited for hosting wedding receptions.

Catering

The most important part of any wedding is undoubtedly the bride and groom, but food is a close second. Book your reception with Temple Inn and Suites, and let us ease your burden by providing a full catering service capable of feeding up to 200 guests. Three different menu themes are provided:

  • Asian Fusion
  • Traditional (Thanksgiving Style)
  • Mexican Fiesta

In addition to traditional catering, we also offer the opportunity to work with our in house cake maker to provide you the perfect wedding cake. Book with Temple Inn and Suites today!

Venue

Temple Inn has 3 large conference rooms with a maximum capacity of 400 guests. These rooms have embedded sound systems paired with large viewing screens on three of the four walls. These are perfect for slideshows and ambient music. Additionally, Temple Inn and Suites an array of 10 multicolor spot lights. Perfect for any dancing or partying that a reception requires. Furthermore, a 15x15 raised platform sits at the front of each room, perfect for seating the bride, groom, and other close friends and family.
