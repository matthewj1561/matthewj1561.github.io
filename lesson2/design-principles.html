My 3 Chosen Principles of Design

Visual Hierarchy

(Work Stack)

When considering visual hierarchy, we must consider what the user's eye is being drawn to. In Workstack's case, they remove all distractions and make the user focus solely on the free trial that is available. Alternatively, the user can view a video that will advertise the product in a positively light. This makes the user experience simple and convenient, which is the goal of all web developers.

Rule of Thirds

(Samsung)

The Rule of Thirds is simple. It states that pictures and graphics are most interesting when the subject of the frame is placed along the lines that split the frame into thirds both horizontal and vertically. In Samsung's site, the first page has a 4 picture carousel, three of them having the picture in the bottom right corner of the picture like in the image above. This shows that Samsung clearly likes to utilize the Rule of Thirds.
