Site Purpose: NGWeather

The purpose of Next Generation Weather is quite simple. The goal of this site is to share the most accurate and reliable weather information with everyone on the internet. Using state of the art tech and complex weather predicting algorithms, NGWeather plans to become the go-to solution when it comes to finding accurate weather information. With the information found on NGWeather, we can help outdoor enthusiasts, everyday suburban moms, and even other weather professionals plan their next outdoor activity on the perfect day.
