Heading Font: Overpass

I enjoyed this look as the site's "attention bringers". This font demands greater presence over Lato, and thus is perfect for headings. However, it is not so dissimilar that it looks out of place.

Paragraph Font: Lato

This font is extremely easy to read and digest. It gets information across easily without unecessary frills. This is exactly what is needed int a main body font.
