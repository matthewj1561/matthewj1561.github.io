Target Audience

NGWeather hopes to reach a massive audience. However, the main consumer of the site will consist of primarily of those who enjoy the outdoors. The more time one spends outside, the more often they will be inclined to know what the day's conditions will be like. However, it is more than likely that the site will serve those who are just commuting to work trying to decide whether to wear a jacket or not. This puts in scope the massive audience this site can cater too.

Personas

Jeffery Smith

Jeffery, an aspiring photographer is trying to stack up his photo gallery. He is a busy man, as he is attending college and working a part time job. Jeffery knows his time is valuable, and so needs to know exactly what days will allow him to get the best outdoor shots. For this service, he goes NGWeather.

Isabella Kitchum

Isabella is an avid RC airplane enthusiast. She loves her hobby so much, she decides to plan a campus wide RC fly day, where all BYU-I students can come fly their RC and have fun with friends. However, she wants to plan the event on a calm day with little wind and sunny skies. If not, someone might crash their aircraft! She understands the importance of good weather information, so she goes to NGWeather to make her decision.

Scenarios

  1. It's nice outside, will it stay that way?
  2. I want to go camping this weekend! What will the weather be like?
  3. How strong was that Texan hurricane again?
  4. When will it get windy enough for me to fly a kite?
  5. What are the best days of the year to go to the beach?
